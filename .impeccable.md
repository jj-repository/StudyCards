## Design Context

### Users

Public release — anyone who studies from markdown notes. Students, self-learners, knowledge workers. Context: focused study sessions, often late at night or in distraction-free environments.

### Brand Personality

Focused, calm, functional. Like a good notebook — quiet, no distractions, lets you concentrate. Content-first. The app should feel invisible — the cards and your progress are what matter.

### Aesthetic Direction

- **Reference**: Anki — functional, no-nonsense, content-first
- **Anti-reference**: Gamified apps (Duolingo), overly decorative dashboards, gradient-heavy modern SaaS
- **Theme**: Dark only — optimized fully, no light mode
- **Tone**: Quiet, confident, utilitarian without being ugly. Think well-made tool, not lifestyle product.

### Design Principles

1. **Content is king** — UI chrome recedes, cards and text take center stage
2. **Calm over stimulating** — muted palette, no visual noise, gentle transitions
3. **Functional density** — show useful info without clutter, respect the user's time
4. **Quiet confidence** — polished details without calling attention to themselves
